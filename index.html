Zodiac Echoes

Zodiac Echoes is a roguelike deckbuilder set in a world of zodiac signs, elemental powers, and spreading corruption. Build powerful synergies, adapt to corrupted enemies, and fight across a shifting front and back row battlefield.

Features

  • Roguelike deckbuilding built around zodiac signs and elemental synergies
  • Zodiac-based characters with unique invocations and flexible build paths
  • Corruption-driven enemy traits that keep encounters unpredictable
  • Front and back row combat with light tactical positioning
  • Craftable relics, potions, and rewards that shape each run
